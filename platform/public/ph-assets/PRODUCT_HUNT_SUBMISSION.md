### Description

**Short (for card):**
SkillTree is your AI-era skill passport — visualize your tech skills as an interactive tree, track progress, and share your growth journey.

**Full (for detail page):**
```
SkillTree transforms how developers track and showcase their skills.

🌳 Visual Skill Tree — See your skills as an interactive DAG (directed acyclic graph) with vibrant nodes and connections

📊 Progress Tracking — XP system, levels, and milestones for 520+ skills across 20 domains

🎴 Share Cards — Generate beautiful 1200×630 PNG cards with QR codes for social sharing

🤝 Community — Leaderboard, endorsements, and skill discovery

🔮 AI-Ready — Open SkillTree Schema for humans and AI agents

Built for developers who want to:
• Visualize their skill growth over time
• Identify learning gaps and next steps
• Share their expertise with the community
• Build an AI-era skills passport

Open source. Free to use. Your skills, your data.
```

### Maker Story (First Comment)

```
Hey Product Hunt! 👋

I'm Alex, and I built SkillTree because I was tired of flat skill lists on LinkedIn that don't capture the depth and connections of what we actually know.

As developers, our skills are interconnected. Learning React leads to Next.js. Understanding TypeScript unlocks advanced patterns. But traditional resumes show none of this.

SkillTree visualizes your skills as a living, growing tree:
• Each node is a skill with 10 levels of mastery
• Connections show prerequisites and learning paths
• XP and badges gamify the growth journey
• Share cards make it beautiful to showcase

The "aha" moment comes when you see your skill tree fill in over time — it's incredibly satisfying to watch your knowledge forest grow. 🌳

This started as a personal tool, but the community response convinced me to build it into something bigger. The Open SkillTree Schema (OSS) is our contribution to making skill data portable across platforms — imagine LinkedIn, GitHub, and AI agents all speaking the same skill language.

**What's next:**
• Browser extension for auto-tracking GitHub activity
• AI coach that suggests learning paths based on your tree gaps
• Integration with learning platforms (Coursera, Egghead, etc.)

Try it out — build your skill tree at https://skilltree.app

I'd love your feedback, brutal honesty welcome! What features would make this indispensable for your career growth?

P.S. — The PH thumbnail is our actual logo rendered as an SVG skill tree. Meta! 🎯
```
