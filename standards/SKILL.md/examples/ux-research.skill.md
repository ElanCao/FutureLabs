# UX Research

UX research focuses on understanding user behaviors, needs, and motivations through observation and feedback.

## Why This Skill Matters

Great products are built on deep user understanding. UX research reduces risk by validating assumptions before costly development.

## Research Methods

- **Interviews**: One-on-one conversations for deep insights
- **Usability Testing**: Observing users complete tasks
- **Surveys**: Quantifying attitudes and behaviors at scale
- **Card Sorting**: Understanding mental models
- **Field Studies**: Contextual inquiry in real environments
- **A/B Testing**: Comparing design variants

## Deliverables

- Research plans
- Discussion guides
- Insight reports
- Personas
- Journey maps
- Usability findings

## Assessment Criteria

- Appropriate method selection
- Rigorous study design
- Unbiased facilitation
- Clear synthesis and findings
- Actionable recommendations
- Stakeholder communication
