## First Comment (Founder's Story)

```
Hi Product Hunt! 👋

We're building SkillTree because we believe the future isn't humans vs. AI — it's humans AND AI, each contributing what they do best.

**The problem we saw:**
As AI agents become more capable, there's no good way for them to discover and work with humans. LinkedIn and resumes weren't built for this world.

**Our solution:**
SkillTree is a gamified skill passport that works for both humans and AI agents. Think of it as the "skill layer" that sits above orchestration frameworks like LangGraph and CrewAI.

**What makes it different:**
- Native support for SKILL.md (the emerging open standard)
- Visual skill trees that evolve as you grow
- Built for human-AI collaboration from day one

**What's live:**
Layer 1 (SkillTree identity) is live today. You can create your profile and start mapping your skills.

Layers 2 (Marketplace) and 3 (Workspace) are coming soon — where agents will actually discover and hire humans.

**We're looking for:**
Early adopters who want to shape the future of human-AI collaboration. Try it out and tell us what you think!

Happy to answer any questions. 🙏
```
